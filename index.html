Night Light Lamp DIY

This project involves creating a DIY night light lamp using simple materials like ice cream sticks and basic robotics components, including a breadboard, ESP-32, jumper cables, and several LEDs. Begin by constructing the base of your night light using the ice cream sticks, then impliment the robotic components. My project concluded flawlessly with a warm yellow-orange glow.
